Jquery Dotify Plugin

Shorten your text using this simple plugin. Dotify wraps parts of your text with two css classes which you can use to stylize to your liking. In our case, the goal is to shorten text and place a "...". So in the css, we do a display:none to hide the extra content, and give you a css class to stylize the "..." to your liking. We are not removing the extra text.

    
        $('.your-class').dotify(
            {
                max: 25,
                dotCharacters: '...'
            }
        );
